Neighbourhood walking research

What do you notice in this street?

You will be shown pictures of neighbourhood streets. There are no right or wrong answers — we are interested in what you notice and how it would affect your own walking.

  • 1 Look around each picture. You can zoom in and drag to move, just like a map.
  • 2 Tap or click anything you notice that would encourage or discourage you from walking there.
  • 3 Tell us what it is and why it matters to you. Mark as many or as few things as you like.
  • 4 At the end of each picture we will ask what you feel is missing.
